System Protocols (FAQ)

Q: How does the Career DNA Engine work?

The matrix evaluates your answers against a weighted algorithm, assigning points to specific career trajectories based on your cognitive and operational preferences to calculate an optimal mathematical match.

Q: Is the salary projection data guaranteed?

No. The projected salaries are statistical aggregations of current market trends. Individual compensation is highly dependent on institutional prestige, skill velocity, and geographic location.

Q: I got matched with a career I didn't expect. Should I change streams?

The engine provides insights, not mandates. Review the "Contingency Pivot (Plan B)" section in the career breakdown to see how your current stream can still align with unexpected roles.

Q: Can I request a new career path to be added?

Yes. Transmit a request via the support form outlining the specific industry, and our analysts will evaluate adding it to the global matrix.
